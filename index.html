Welcome to the "Heidelberg Makerspace" community of Makers in and around Heidelberg!

Are you looking for a space? Depending on your interests, there are a lot of different options
to choose from. We can't tell you what will best suit your needs but here's an alphabetical list
of the places we know of - if something sounds interesting to you, just go check it out!

Maker Resources in Heidelberg

  • Action House in Bergheim (artists' coworking space with public events)
  • Ceramic-Atelier in Handschuhsheim
  • CoMakingSpace - Community Makerspace in Pfaffengrund (wiki)
  • DAI Makerspace by Bismarckplatz (planned to move into "Begeisterhaus" eventually)
  • Karlstorkino/Medienforum e.V. (open photo lab)
  • konvisionär e.V.
  • NoName e.V. (Chaostreff Heidelberg)
  • open art studio in St. Albert's church
  • URRmEL (students' communal bike repair shop) on Neuenheimer Feld campus
  • Werkraum in Kirchhoff-Institute for Physics (for students) (wiki)
  • WerkstattSchule Heidelberg

Around Heidelberg

  • Handwerkerhof in Billigheim
  • Makerspace Rhein-Neckar in Ludwigshafen
  • RaumZeitLabor in Mannheim (wiki)
